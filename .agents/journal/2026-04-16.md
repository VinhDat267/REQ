## Outcome
- The requirement package is now materially closer to a real small-restaurant POS workflow:
  - bank-transfer QR is treated as manual confirmation
  - Takeaway cannot reach the kitchen unpaid
  - add-on orders can be attached to an active dine-in table
  - Pickup no-show is no longer silently glossed over
  - menu history is protected by archive/deactivate behavior
  - customer walk-away and repeated payment-failure scenarios are now explicit instead of assumed
  - late callbacks, duplicate payment signals, wrong handoff, partial fulfilment failure, and shift-close reconciliation are now explicit operational rules rather than hidden assumptions
